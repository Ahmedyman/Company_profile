About

Egyptian Technology for water treatment Company

Founded in 2018, Egyptian Technology for Water Treatment (ETCo) specializes in providing high-performance chemicals and advanced treatment solutions for a variety of applications. Our services cover boilers, cooling systems, process side .protection for industrial plants Our expertise combines cutting-edge technology, years of experience, and a strong commitment to delivering reliable and effective solutions. We offer a comprehensive range of products, including scale and corrosion inhibitors, microbiocides, defoamers, and cleaning chemicals designed with .environmentally friendly formulas At ETCo, we prioritize quality, innovation, and environmental sustainability. We are dedicated to creating customized treatment programs that ensure efficient operation, preserve natural resources, and reduce environmental impact. Our programs are tailored to the specific needs of feed water and temperature conditions, supported by rigorous water analysis .and exceptional after-sales service

Vision and Mission
